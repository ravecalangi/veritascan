What is Veritascan

AI built to fight misinformation.

Veritascan is an AI-powered fact-checking tool that helps you verify news, articles, and images in seconds. Simply paste a URL or text and our system cross-references it against live sources to give you an honest credibility verdict, while also checking whether the content or image is AI-generated.

Built in alignment with UN Sustainable Development Goal 16 — promoting access to information and protecting fundamental freedoms.
